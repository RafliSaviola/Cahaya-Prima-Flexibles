About Us

Founded in 2017, PT Cahaya Prima Flexibles operates as a subsidiary of PT. Mutiaracahaya Plastindo, a company with over 30 years of experience in the plastic packaging industry. We cater to the market's demand for high-quality flexible packaging solutions.

Equipped with advanced 10-color printing machines, we meet the complexities of flexible packaging while providing alternatives in larger sizes, including plastic sacks, kraft paper, and non-woven materials.

With our extensive expertise across multiple industries, we are committed to delivering packaging solutions that embody three key principles: Protectiveness, Attractiveness, and Cost-Effectiveness. Trust PT Cahaya Prima Flexibles to provide the ideal packaging that meets your needs..
